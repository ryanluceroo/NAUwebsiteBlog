Downtown Flagstaff

Things To Do:

Downtown Flagstaff has a rich history going as far back as 1897. In 1897 the Weatherford Hotel was established by John W. Weatherford and is pictured on the right. This hotel is still operational and
is known by most of the locals for the new years event that they put on every year.

Downtown Hotels

Other Things To Do:

Some other things to do in warm weather include: Check out Heritage Square or Wheeler Park,
enjoy live music, visit the bars and enjoy the nice weather!
When the weather is cold you can: Sit down at one of the cafes and enjoy a hot drink, visit the
Weatherford hotel and Monte Vista hotel, have warm drinks by the fire pit and check out the local shops!
