Other Fun Activities

Some Other Fun Activities That Flagstaff Has To Offer

A brief summary of what Snowbowl has to offer.

Lockett Meadows

Lockett Meadows is a world famous hiking trail. It is home to the beautiful Aspen Trees that turn bright yellow in the fall.

Disc Golf

Believe it or not, disc golf is a fairly common outdoor activity in Flagstaff and with the beautiful scenery and nice city-run courses it makes for a good weekend activity.

Sedona

Sedona is only an hour drive south of Flagstaff. Sedona is home to beautiful rich-red rock and varoius different activities.
